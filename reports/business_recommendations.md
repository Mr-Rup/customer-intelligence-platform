# Business Recommendations Report

## Overview

This report translates analytical findings into actionable retention strategies,
prioritized by expected business impact and implementation cost.

---

## Priority 1: Premium Retention Package

**Target**: High CLV + High Churn Risk customers

**Action Items**:
- Assign dedicated account manager
- Offer personalized retention incentive (15-20% discount on annual contract)
- Schedule quarterly service review calls
- Provide priority technical support access

**Expected Impact**: High
**Investment Level**: $$$
**Timeline**: Immediate

---

## Priority 2: Contract Migration Program

**Target**: Month-to-month customers with elevated churn risk

**Action Items**:
- Offer 15% discount for switching to annual contract
- Send comparison email showing total annual savings
- Highlight added benefits of longer-term contracts
- Create urgency with limited-time offer window

**Expected Impact**: High
**Investment Level**: $$
**Timeline**: Within 30 days

**Rationale**: Month-to-month contracts have a 43% churn rate vs 3% for two-year contracts.
Contract migration is the single highest-impact intervention.

---

## Priority 3: Security Bundle Upgrade

**Target**: Fiber optic customers without Online Security or Tech Support

**Action Items**:
- Offer free 3-month trial of Online Security + Tech Support bundle
- Emphasize protection value specific to fiber customers
- Follow up at end of trial with retention offer

**Expected Impact**: Medium
**Investment Level**: $$
**Timeline**: Within 60 days

**Rationale**: Fiber customers without security services churn at 42%+.
Adding security services drops churn rate to ~15%.

---

## Priority 4: New Customer Onboarding Program

**Target**: Customers in first 12 months (the "danger zone")

**Action Items**:
- Trigger guided onboarding program at signup
- Assign onboarding specialist for first 90 days
- Schedule check-in calls at 30, 60, and 90 days
- Send proactive usage tips and service recommendations

**Expected Impact**: Medium
**Investment Level**: $
**Timeline**: Ongoing

**Rationale**: 50% of all churn occurs in the first year.
Strong onboarding can significantly impact first-year retention.

---

## Priority 5: Payment Method Transition

**Target**: Electronic check users with elevated churn risk

**Action Items**:
- Offer $5/month discount for switching to automatic bank transfer or credit card
- Emphasize convenience and autopay benefits
- Simplify the transition process

**Expected Impact**: Medium
**Investment Level**: $
**Timeline**: Within 60 days

**Rationale**: Electronic check users churn at 45%, the highest of any payment method.

---

## Priority 6: Service Enrichment

**Target**: Customers with 1-2 services and medium churn risk

**Action Items**:
- Recommend complementary services based on current subscriptions
- Offer first month free for any new service addition
- Create service bundle packages at discounted rates

---

## Priority 7: Loyalty Recognition

**Target**: High CLV + Low Risk (loyal VIP customers)

**Action Items**:
- Send loyalty appreciation communications
- Offer exclusive upgrade or early access to new services
- Include in VIP loyalty program with perks

---
